How It Works


Amplive allows live broadcasters to drive traffic to their digital content in real-time and grow their community (expand their customer base) by promoting their videos live on the Web's most visited sites.

1. Find Your Audience


Reach the right people at the right time


We place your video inside a custom interactive ad unit, target your key audiences, and distribute your video to any number of websites to reach maximum tune-in as soon as you launch your video.

2. Grow Your Audience


Learn about your audience during your broadcast


Next we capture viewer insights and analyze the actions they take. Detailed analytics are viewable on your dashboard and provide a complete view of audience engagement during your campaign.

3. Engage Your Audience


Optimize your marketing efforts & drive results


Our re-marketing tools allow you to segment specific leads and later, re-engage them to reduce promotional costs and increase ROI.
